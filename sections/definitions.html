1. Variable Definitions & Theory

Path A: The Growth Path

  • AI-Enabled Competence (IV): The perception that AI tools enhance efficiency and skill, satisfying the SDT need for Competence.
  • Harmonious Passion (Mediator): Entrepreneurship is internalized autonomously into the student's identity. They engage because they love it.

Path B: The Pressure Path

  • AI-Induced Dependency (IV): The feeling that one cannot function without AI, thwarting the SDT need for Autonomy and creating external pressure.
  • Obsessive Passion (Mediator): Entrepreneurship is pursued due to internal or external pressure (e.g., fear of missing out, status).

Contextual Factors

  • Student Hardiness (Moderator): A personality trait comprising Control, Commitment, and Challenge. High hardiness helps Vietnamese students navigate transitional stress.
  • Entrepreneurial Intention (DV): The outcome variable representing the conscious resolve to start a business.
